Our Story

A Piece of Mexico
On Wheels

El Chilaquil was born from a dream to bring the vibrant, authentic flavors of Mexican street food to London, Ontario.

Founded by Geiler Puerto, who grew up in Mexico surrounded by the sights, smells, and tastes of authentic street food. Every recipe carries the soul of Mexican tradition — from our slow-cooked barbacoa to our hand-rolled churros.

We don't take shortcuts. Every dish is handcrafted with fresh ingredients and traditional techniques, because real flavor can't be faked.

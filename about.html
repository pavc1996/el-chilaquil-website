The Founder

Meet Geiler Puerto

El Chilaquil was born from a dream to bring the vibrant, authentic flavors of Mexican street food to London, Ontario.

Geiler grew up in Mexico surrounded by the sights, smells, and tastes of authentic street food. From bustling mercados to family kitchens, food was always at the center of life, community, and celebration.

Every recipe at El Chilaquil carries the soul of Mexican tradition — from our slow-cooked barbacoa to our hand-rolled churros. We don't take shortcuts. Every dish is handcrafted with fresh ingredients and traditional techniques, because real flavor can't be faked.

What started as a passion project has grown into London's favourite food truck, catering hundreds of events and serving thousands of happy customers across Ontario.

The Truck

Meet El Chilaquil

Our vibrant food truck is hard to miss — and even harder to forget once you've tried the food.

Decked out in colourful Mexican art featuring sugar skulls, guitars, and our mascot chihuahua, the El Chilaquil truck brings the party wherever it goes. We're fully equipped for on-site cooking, so every taco, quesadilla, and churro is made fresh right in front of you.

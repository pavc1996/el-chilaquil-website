The Founder

Meet Geiler Puerto

Geiler grew up in Mérida, Yucatán, in a household that blended culinary traditions from two Mexican regions.

His father, originally from Hunucmá, transitioned from farming to the food business — selling tacos out of their home in the 1990s before later entering the meat industry in Cancún. He always harboured one dream: seeing his son open a restaurant in Canada.

His mother came from Mexico City, introducing central Mexican flavors that merged with Yucatán cooking in their home kitchen. Together, they instilled a belief that has become El Chilaquil's guiding philosophy: "a full belly means a happy heart."

In 2013, Geiler moved to London, Ontario and pursued culinary education at Fanshawe College. After years of professional kitchen experience, he built his vision for authentic Mexican street food — and El Chilaquil was born. Every taco, every churro, every plate connects to generations of food, family, and cultural pride.

The Truck

Meet El Chilaquil

Our vibrant food truck is hard to miss — and even harder to forget once you've tried the food.

Decked out in colourful Mexican art featuring sugar skulls, guitars, and our mascot chihuahua, the El Chilaquil truck brings the party wherever it goes. We're fully equipped for on-site cooking, so every taco, quesadilla, and churro is made fresh right in front of you.
